Dynamics - Simulation of Motion of Earth and Moon Around the Sun



Project A: Simulation of Motion of Earth and Moon Around the Sun. Created Matlab script that used leapfrog integration method for numerically integrating differential equations. Calculated force between bodies using Newton's law of universal gravitation.

Project B: Motion of a Binary Star System. Binary star system of two stars with radius R and 5R and of the same density. The center of mass of the system is fixed while their maximum distance is approximately100R.

Project C: Motion of a Trinary Star System. Same system as Project B, but a third star of radius R 4 rotates about the star of radius R at a distance 10R . All stars have the same density
